\subsection{Bayesian Analysis}\label{bayesian-analysis}

\subsubsection{Contrast Setup}\label{contrast-setup}

We use Helmert-like orthonormal contrasts for the 3-level factor
\texttt{Condition2}. Hypothesis: We want to compare levels in a
structured way. Assumed Levels: \texttt{attractorMatch},
\texttt{noMatch}, \texttt{targetMatch}.

We define a custom contrast matrix. Contrast 1: \texttt{attractorMatch}
vs \texttt{targetMatch} (ignoring \texttt{noMatch}). Contrast 2:
\texttt{noMatch} vs Average of (\texttt{attractorMatch} \&
\texttt{targetMatch}).
